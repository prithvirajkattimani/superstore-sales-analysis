# Superstore Sales Analysis

## Project Objective
Analyze sales data to understand trends, growth patterns, category performance, and regional performance, and give business-driven recommendations.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook

## Steps Performed
1. Loaded cleaned Superstore dataset  
2. Analyzed yearly sales trends  
3. Removed incomplete year (2019)  
4. Category-wise sales and growth analysis  
5. Region-wise sales and growth analysis  
6. Business insights from every chart  

## Key Insights
- Sales grew consistently from 2015 to 2018  
- West region shows fastest growth  
- Technology drives big growth but is volatile  
- Office Supplies gives stable growth  
- Furniture is slowing every year  
- South region is underperforming  
- “Unknown” category is unreliable and needs cleaning  

## Final Business Recommendations
- Invest more in West region  
- Push technology products in high-growth regions  
- Use office supplies as steady revenue base  
- Fix or reduce focus on furniture  
- Improve South region strategy  
- Remove “Unknown” category from future data
